Research

My research focuses on the automation of cybersecurity operations using artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on trustworthy, explainable, and standards-aligned decision-support systems. I am particularly interested in bridging academic research with real operational environments, including SOC and OT/ICS contexts.

Research Interests

  • AI-driven cybersecurity automation
  • Alert triage and false-positive reduction
  • Knowledge graphs and ontologies for security reasoning
  • Access control policy verification in hybrid IT environments
  • OT / ICS security analytics
